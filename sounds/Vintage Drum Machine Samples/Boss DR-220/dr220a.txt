Boss DR 220 A

This drumbox from the mid 80's contains 
static samples, only volume is adjustable.
Just sample and play back, then you have 
an original sounding DR 220 A.

BD - Bassdrum
SN - Snaredrum
RIM - Rimshot
HCP - Handclap
CH - Closed HiHat
OH - Open HiHat
RCY - RideCymbal
CCY - CrashCymbal
LT - Low Tom
MT - Mid Tom
HT - High Tom
